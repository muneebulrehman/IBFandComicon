International Book Fair and Comicon 2022

Comicon and book fair bringing all the fans from over the world together. 7 day event with lots of activities, award ceremony and interactions with some of the most successful writers of our time. Book Fairs serve to promote the habit of reading in people. By offering a wide selection of books and related material throughout the duration of event, we wish to give you an opportunity to read by choice and not by compulsion.
